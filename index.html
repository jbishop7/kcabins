Your Retreat Awaits

Kincardine Cabin Retreat is hidden amongst giant cedar trees overlooking the shores of Lake Huron. The property is less than 3 minutes by foot away from downtown Kincardine, the Lighthouse, and pristine Blue Flag beaches. This rustic property has a prominent place in local history stemming from its roots as a tourist retreat from 1946 - 1970, when it was known as HILORCORT CABINS.


This unique rental property is perfect for a group or for family reunions, weddings, and special events. It is best suited for a maximum of 12 people and the weekly rate includes 4 cabins, stone cottage, cook house, bath house, deck area and exclusive use of the west end of the grounds. In addition, the Coach House is available as a separate rental option.


Please take a moment and browse through the many photos that capture the beauty and essence of this rental property. Photos from the early 1940s show original owner and WWII vet, John A. “Skip” Reynolds, building the log cabins. More recent photos showcase the extensive renovations, spectacular views, grounds, gardens and many amenities. Book your next vacation or get away at Kincardine Cabin Retreat, where our motto is “A vacation is having nothing to do and all day to do it!”

Cabins

Coach House

The cabins are located on the back side of the property (West) with a view of Lake Huron and its beautiful sunsets. There are 4 cabins included in the rental:
Cabin 1 is 250 sq ft with a Queen size bed, two piece bath, and sitting area by the original fireplace. There is also a dumb waiter that works by a rope pully that was used to keep food cold prior to refridgerators becoming popular. Lastly, this cabin includes a ceiling fan and electric heat.
Cabin 2 is the same as Cabin 1 except that there is a wood burning fireplace.
Cabin 3 is connected to Cabin 4 and is used as a Gathering Room with a pullout couch, futon, cable tv, wood burning fireplace, and electric heat.
Cabin 4 is 250 sq ft with a King size bed, two piece bathroom, dumb waiter, ceiling fan, and electric heat.
The Coach House is located near the front of the property by the Main House. This is a 600 sq ft self contained rental unit complete with a Queen size bed, kitchenette, and three piece bathroom. The Coach House also includes a gas fireplace, big screen TV, and a leather couch, love seat, and chair.
